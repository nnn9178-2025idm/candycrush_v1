Built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS. Characters are emoji for portability.

How to Play

Tap groups of 2 or more adjacent same-colored animals to pop them. Gravity drops tiles and new ones appear.

  • Pop 5+ to create a 🚀 Rocket (clears a full row or column).
  • Pop 7+ to create a 💣 Bomb (clears a 3×3 area).
  • Finish the goals within the moves limit to win the level.
